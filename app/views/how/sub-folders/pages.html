Pages

Files inside a sub-folder called Pages become pages instead of posts. Links to each page appear on your site’s menu. To change the order of the pages on your menu or add external links, open the template editor from your site's dashboard and edit the menu links.

Hidden pages

You can hide a page from your site’s menu with the Menu metadata. 

Menu: no
Link: /hidden

This page will not appear on your site‘s menu. It is accessible only by its URL, which is ‘/hidden’.

Create a landing page

You can create a landing page with the Link metadata. 

Link: /

This page will replace your site‘s homepage. 

If you still want your readers to access the list of your posts, consider adding a link to ‘/page/1’ to your site’s menu. You can edit your site's menu links in the template editor on the dashboard.

External links

You can add external links to your menu in the template editor on your site's dashboard.

Edit the order of the links on your menu

You can change the order of the links on your menu in the template editor on your site's dashboard.
