Analytics

Blot does not have a built-in analytics system for tracking visitors to your site. However, you can install any of the following analytics services with a few clicks on the External services of your site's dashboard.

  • Google Analytics
  • Heap
  • Clicky
  • Simple Analytics
  • Fathom
  • Plausible

Switching analytics service

You can replace the analytics service on your site with a few clicks on the External services of your site's dashboard.
